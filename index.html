Facebook Performance

Facebook continues to be a powerful platform for reaching new audiences, with both branches showing explosive growth in May.

HPC (Main Branch)

Massive Reach to New Guests

Content reached 34,500 people - a huge 4,600% increase! Almost all views (97%) came from people who don't follow our page yet, showing our content is being discovered by new potential guests.

Growing Audience

We gained 44 new followers with no unfollows, meaning people who discover our page like what they see enough to stay connected.

HPT (Tuuti Branch)

Strong Growth Trajectory

HPT reached 29,100 people - a 2,500% increase. Like HPC, 96% of viewers were discovering our page for the first time, showing great potential for attracting new guests.

Engagement Strength

With 216 interactions, HPT actually had more engagement than HPC. Our followers are particularly engaged, showing strong connection with our content.

Facebook Key Takeaway

Both locations are successfully using Facebook to reach new audiences. The challenge now is converting these viewers into followers and eventually guests. We should consider adding special offers for first-time visitors in our Facebook posts.

Instagram Performance

Instagram serves a different purpose than Facebook - it's where your existing followers engage most deeply with our content.

HPC (Main Branch)

Loyal Followers

While we reached 5,500 people (a massive 900% increase), what's impressive is that our existing followers accounted for 67% of all interactions. This shows our current guests love our content.

Steady Growth

We gained 42 new followers but lost 22, resulting in a net gain of 20. This suggests while we're attracting new followers, some content might not be meeting expectations.

HPT (Tuuti Branch)

Growth from Small Base

While numbers are smaller than other platforms, we achieved impressive growth percentages - 283% increase in reach and 238% increase in interactions. This shows great momentum building.

Community Focus

69% of our views and 55% of interactions come from existing followers - higher than HPC. This makes Instagram HPT's community hub where loyal guests engage most.

Instagram Strategy Insight

Instagram is where our existing guests feel most connected to our brand. For HPC, we focus on converting new viewers into followers. For HPT, leverage our strong follower engagement to encourage user-generated content and reviews.

TikTok shows the most exciting growth potential, especially for reaching younger audiences.

HPC (Main Branch)

Exceptional Growth

HPC's TikTok is on fire! With 9,463 video views (389% increase) and 791 likes, this is our fastest-growing platform. Most impressive is the 777 net new followers - far exceeding other platforms.

Viral Potential

Content performed exceptionally well. Analyzing what worked on this month could unlock consistent viral success.

HPT (Tuuti Branch)

Local Discovery Hub

Growth Opportunity

TikTok Advantage
