Facebook Performance

Facebook continues to be a powerful platform for reaching new audiences, with both branches showing explosive growth in May.

HPC (Main Branch)

Massive Reach to New Guests

Your content reached 34,500 people - a huge 4,600% increase! Almost all views (97%) came from people who don't follow your page yet, showing your content is being discovered by new potential guests.

Growing Audience

You gained 44 new followers with no unfollows, meaning people who discover your page like what they see enough to stay connected.

HPT (Tuuti Branch)

Strong Growth Trajectory

HPT reached 29,100 people - a 2,500% increase. Like HPC, 96% of viewers were discovering your page for the first time, showing great potential for attracting new guests.

Engagement Strength

With 216 interactions, HPT actually had more engagement than HPC. Your followers are particularly engaged, showing strong connection with your content.

Facebook Key Takeaway

Both locations are successfully using Facebook to reach new audiences. The challenge now is converting these viewers into followers and eventually guests. Consider adding special offers for first-time visitors in your Facebook posts.

Instagram Performance

Instagram serves a different purpose than Facebook - it's where your existing followers engage most deeply with your content.

HPC (Main Branch)

Loyal Followers

While you reached 5,500 people (a massive 900% increase), what's impressive is that your existing followers accounted for 67% of all interactions. This shows your current guests love your content.

Steady Growth

You gained 42 new followers but lost 22, resulting in a net gain of 20. This suggests while you're attracting new followers, some content might not be meeting expectations.

HPT (Tuuti Branch)

Growth from Small Base

While numbers are smaller than other platforms, you achieved impressive growth percentages - 283% increase in reach and 238% increase in interactions. This shows great momentum building.

Community Focus

69% of your views and 55% of interactions come from existing followers - higher than HPC. This makes Instagram HPT's community hub where loyal guests engage most.

Instagram Strategy Insight

Instagram is where your existing guests feel most connected to your brand. For HPC, focus on converting new viewers into followers. For HPT, leverage your strong follower engagement to encourage user-generated content and reviews.

TikTok Performance

TikTok shows the most exciting growth potential, especially for reaching younger audiences.

HPC (Main Branch)

Exceptional Growth

HPC's TikTok is on fire! With 9,463 video views (389% increase) and 791 likes, this is your fastest-growing platform. Most impressive is the 777 net new followers - far exceeding other platforms.

Viral Potential

Content from May 16 and May 25 performed exceptionally well. Analyzing what worked on these dates could unlock consistent viral success.

HPT (Tuuti Branch)

Local Discovery Hub

Your TikTok is being found through local searches like "best hotels in Sirisia" and "hunters paradise bungorna menu". This shows potential guests are actively searching for what you offer.

Growth Opportunity

While you have 2,100 views (46% increase), engagement is low with only 89 likes. Your content is being discovered but not deeply engaging viewers yet.

TikTok Advantage

TikTok is your most effective platform for reaching new, younger audiences. For HPC, maintain momentum with consistent posting. For HPT, create content that answers local search queries and showcases what makes your location unique.
